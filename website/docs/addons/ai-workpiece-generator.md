# AI Workpiece Generator

Create laser-cut designs from text descriptions using artificial intelligence.

## Overview

The AI Workpiece Generator addon lets you create designs by simply
describing what you want. Type a description like "a simple star shape"
or "a gear with 8 teeth" and the AI will generate a workpiece ready for
your laser cutter.

![AI Workpiece Generator Dialog](/screenshots/addon-ai-workpiece-generator.png)

## Requirements

Before using this addon, you need to configure an AI provider. See
[AI Provider](../application-settings/ai-provider) for setup instructions.

## Using the Generator

1. Open the AI Workpiece Generator from the **Tools** menu
2. Enter a description of the shape you want to create
3. Specify the dimensions in millimeters
4. Click **Generate** to create your design
5. The generated workpiece will appear on your canvas

## Tips for Best Results

- **Be specific**: "a 5-pointed star with sharp points" works better than
  "a star"
- **Keep it simple**: The AI works best with straightforward geometric
  designs
- **Specify details**: Include important details like number of teeth,
  corners, or symmetry
- **Use physical descriptions**: Describe the shape, not the cutting
  parameters

## Example Prompts

- "a simple heart shape"
- "a gear with 12 teeth"
- "a hexagon with a circular hole in the center"
- "an arrow pointing right"
- "a snowflake with 6 arms"
